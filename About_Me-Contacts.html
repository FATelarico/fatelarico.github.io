About Me

I was born in Naples, Southern Italy. I am fluent in Italian, English and Bulgarian. In 2023 I started my PhD in Statistics at the University of Ljubljana (Slovenia). Since 2021, I have been working at the University of Ljubljana (Faculty of Social Sciences) in various research-related positions.Besides being a researcher, I give occasional guest lectures and speak regularly at international conferences on these topics.

Between 2015 and 2017 I won several prizes in nation-wide literary contests. So, I decided to start write for websites and magazines about culture, society and politics in South-Eastern Europe and the former USSR. Eventually, I found myself freelancing as a risk and political-economy analyst for Global Risk Insights, Modern Diplomacy, and Lone Conservative. Currently I am actively cooperating with the European Liberal Forum (a think tank related to the Alliance of Liberal and Democrats in Europe, ALDE) and the European Student Think Tank (ESTT).
